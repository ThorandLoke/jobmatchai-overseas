Tips for ATS-Friendly Resumes

1. Use standard section headers (Experience, Education, Skills)
2. Include keywords from the job description
3. Use standard fonts like Arial, Calibri, or Garamond
4. Keep it to 1-2 pages, save as PDF format
5. Quantify your achievements (e.g., "Increased sales by 30%")
